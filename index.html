About Me

Data Science, Engineering, Economics and Financial Analytics are my passions. My name is Neil Shastry and I have been working in the field of Data and Analytics for over 10 years. I did my masters in Economics with applied Mathematics and Statistics. Numbers have always been my passion and I have been working in retail analytics for HSBC for nearly 9 years primarily applying CRM, Data Science and recently cloud enterprise and agile architecture models to optimize and personalize customer experience. My primary areas of focus have been wealth and insurance products. I have also worked as a digital product manager and previously supported marketing teams with Market-Mix Models and digital acquisition strategies.
